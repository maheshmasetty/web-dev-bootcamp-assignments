SECTION1

A bus organization consists of 7 CPU registers. The output of each register is connected to two multiplexers to form the two buses A and B. The selection lines in each multiplexer select one register or the input data for the particular bus. The A and B buses form the inputs to a common arithmetic logic unit(ALU).
